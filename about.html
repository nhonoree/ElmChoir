About Our Choir

Our History

Founded in 2010, Harmony Choir began as a small group of passionate singers in a local community center. Over the years, we've grown to become one of the region's most celebrated choral groups...

Our journey has taken us from humble beginnings to prestigious concert halls, but our mission remains the same: to share the joy of choral music with everyone.

Meet Our Conductor

Dr. Sarah Johnson

With over 20 years of experience in choral direction, Dr. Johnson brings a wealth of knowledge and passion to Harmony Choir...

Her innovative approach to choral music has earned her numerous awards and the respect of musicians nationwide.
